# Accordion

The `<AccordionGroup>` shows a summary of the panel content and acts as the control for expanding and collapsing. By default, `AccordionGroup` includes a toggle icon at the end of the header to indicate the expansion state. The `AccordionItem` shows the content of the accordion when expanded.

## Guidelines

- Use accordions to simplify long content pages and minimize scrolling.
- Avoid using accordions if users need to access content under several accordions frequently.
- Accordions are ideal for content-heavy pages where users need to focus on specific sections.
- They can guide users through processes by showing relevant information and hiding distractions.
- Commonly used in multistep flows to make the experience more manageable.
- Use `className="sol-panel"` on the accordion container to apply panel styling.
